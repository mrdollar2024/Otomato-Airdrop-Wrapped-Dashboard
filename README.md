# Otomato-Airdrop-Wrapped-Dashboard
Track your airdrop history, wallet rankings, achievements, and on-chain activity with a personalized Wrapped dashboard.
# Features

* View your Airdrop Wrapped statistics and yearly summary
* Check wallet rankings and leaderboard positions
* Explore historical airdrop eligibility and rewards
* Track on-chain activity across multiple protocols
* Unlock achievements, badges, and milestones
* Analyze your DeFi footprint and participation history
* Compare your performance with other users
* Generate and share personalized Wrapped cards
* Discover potential future airdrop opportunities
* Monitor wallet growth and ecosystem engagement
